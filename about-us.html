About Us

Artifice is a Woodlawn-based tech center, founded in 2013, with a mission to bring tech and entrepreneurial education to students in our community. Many people have contributed to make Artifice a thriving tech center. To learn more about what we do and to view our courses and projects, visit our blog. We're always looking for more people with interests in tech and education to help us build an even more awesome community!
